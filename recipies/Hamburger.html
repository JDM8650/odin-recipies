Hamburger

This recipe will detail how to make a Hamburger

Ingredients

  • 2 burger buns
  • Hamburger meat
  • Cheese (optional)
  • Vegetables (optional)
  • condiments

Steps

  1. Shape and season the burger meat
  2. Grill the burger meat
  3. Place the cooked meat on the flat bun
  4. Place the optional toppings on the meat
  5. Place the top bun on the toppings

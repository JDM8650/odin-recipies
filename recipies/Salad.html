Salad

This recipe will detail how to make a Salad bowl

Ingredients

  • lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Cheese
  • Dressing

Steps

  1. Tear the lettuce of the head and place in a bowl
  2. Dice the tomatoes into small chunks and place in bowl
  3. Place the grated cheese into the bowl
  4. Mix together
  5. Add dressing on top
